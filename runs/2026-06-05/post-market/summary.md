4. **SpaceX IPO could be a massive sentiment catalyst.** $150B demand vs $75B raise. Pricing June 11, debut June 12. If the IPO pops, it could reignite risk appetite and divert attention from the macro headwinds — or it could suck liquidity from existing positions.

5. **ECB hiking Thursday could paradoxically help.** If the ECB hikes 25bp, EUR/USD could strengthen, weakening the dollar and providing relief to commodities and EM — but it also reinforces the global tightening narrative.
